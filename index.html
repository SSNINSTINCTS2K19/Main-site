About Us

Whom do you trust at the most important points in your life? Who helps you in making the right choice everytime? It is your INSTINCTS. Trust those instincts and follow them to SSN College of Engineering, where every ounce of your talent is rewarded, regardless of who you are. This time, Instincts 2k19 promises to take you around the world, show you all the 8 wonders of the world and expose the unexplored strengths in you. From Music, Art, Dance, Knowledge to your Gaming prowess, every aspect of you is put on a pedestal. Banish your shyness and embrace glory. If you are still wondering about the Eighth wonder of the world, its none other than you. Welcome to SSN Instincts 2k19, the festival that celebrates you.
